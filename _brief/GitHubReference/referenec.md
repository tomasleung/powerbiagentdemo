Ask when audience is unclear:

Who is this report primarily for?

Recommended choices:

Executives / leadership — concise KPIs, trends, risks, decisions
Analysts — exploration, drilldowns, comparisons, tables
Operators — monitoring, exceptions, status, action queues
External audience — polished story, guided narrative, minimal slicers
Enthusiasts / fans — magazine-style narrative, rich visuals, rankings
Then ask only if the job-to-be-done is still unclear:

What should the report help them do?

Recommended choices:

Understand the overall story
Track performance
Find outliers or opportunities
Compare entities or segments
Explore individual records/profiles
Prepare for a recurring business review
